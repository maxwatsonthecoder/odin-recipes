Easy Iced Mocha

This is my favorite easy iced mocha recipe. I make it everyday! Warning: Once you try this, you'll never want to go to Starbucks again!

Prep Time: 5 minutes

Yeild: 3 Iced Coffees

Ingredients

  • 4 cups brewed coffee
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa powder (dutch processed preferred)
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/4 teasponn cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 cup vanilla almond milk
  • 3 tablespoons heavy cream

Directions

  1. Mix together dry ingredients in a large bowl.
  2. Pour in brewed coffee.
  3. Add almond milk and heavy cream.
  4. Allow to sit for about 5 minutes to give the cream time to incorporate. This step is important for full flavor and creamy texture.
  5. Pour over ice and enjoy!
